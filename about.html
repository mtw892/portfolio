Who I Am

Hello! I’m Mason, a web designer and front end developer who loves bringing ideas to life on the web. I specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m all about creating websites that not only look great but are super functional and easy to use. There’s nothing better than taking a concept and turning it into something real that people can connect with. I’m currently studying web development and design at Harrisburg Area Community College to keep sharpening my skills and stay ahead of the curve. I’m always up for a challenge, whether it’s figuring out a tricky bit of code or learning something new to level up my projects. I currently live in Hershey, Pennsylvania, where I've spent most of my life. One day, I hope to relocate and embrace a peaceful life by the sea. I cherish quality time with my friends and family and often find myself daydreaming about my next big goal. If you’re looking for someone who’s easy to work with, outgoing, loves a good challenge, and takes pride in getting the details just right, let’s talk!
